🎮 How to Play

  • 🌍Face 17 SDG questions — one per Global Goal.
  • ⏱️You have 30 seconds per question. Don't let the clock run out!
  • ✅Pick the 1 correct answer from the 3×3 grid of options.
  • 🪢Correct answer → rope pulls toward WINNING side. Wrong → toward DANGER!
  • 🏆Pull the rope all the way to WIN. Score as many points as you can!

⚔️ Two Player Rules

  • 🔴Player 1 (Red): Press R to buzz in!
  • 🔵Player 2 (Blue): Press B to buzz in!
  • ⚡Whoever buzzes first gets to answer. Speed matters!
  • ⏰Wrong answer or timeout → chance passes + time bonus added to opponent.
  • 🪢Pull the rope to your side to claim VICTORY!
