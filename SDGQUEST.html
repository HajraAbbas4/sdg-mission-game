How to Play!

Master the SDG Space Quest!

1
🎯 Aim & Shoot
Move your mouse to aim the glowing arrow, then click to fire!
2
🫧 Match 3 or More
Pop 3+ bubbles of the same SDG colour. More = more points!
3
🔄 Swap Bubbles
Click the Swap button (bottom-left) or right-click to switch your current and next bubble.
4
⭐ Earn Stars
Stars fill as you clear the board. Clear 33% for ⭐ · 66% for ⭐⭐ · 100% for ⭐⭐⭐
5
⏱ Watch the Timer!
The timer bubble and shot counter bubble are shown inside the game area at the bottom. Don't let time run out!
💡

Pro tip: Bubbles disconnected from the top wall fall down — earn bonus points by breaking clusters!
