HOW TO PLAY
TURN17  —  TURN, EXPLORE, AND UNLOCK THE FUTURE
01🕹️
Navigate the Maze
Use the Arrow Keys (↑ ↓ ← →) on your keyboard to navigate through the space maze. Find your path and keep moving!
02⏱️
Beat the Clock
You start with 30 seconds. The timer counts down as you explore. Collect badges along the way to keep your time alive!
03🏅
Collect SDG Badges
There are 17 badges scattered across the maze. Each one reveals a UN Sustainable Development Goal and rewards you with +20 seconds.
04⚠️
When Time Runs Out
If the timer hits zero, the game ends. Choose to try again or go home — collect more badges next time to extend your mission!
05🏠
Reach Home to Win
Navigate through the maze and reach 🏠 Home before time runs out. The more badges you collect, the more time you earn to find your way!
06🌍
Learn the 17 SDGs
Every badge is a lesson. After the game, use 🔒 Locked Badges to read any SDG goals you missed — because every turn unlocks the future.
